Key Concepts

These core ideas form the foundation of Leo Legacy Trading. Each card includes a small practice suggestion so the concept becomes something you do, not just read.

1. Market Structure

Identify highs, lows and breaks in structure across timeframes.

Practice: Label structure on screenshots daily, marking HH, HL, LH, LL.

2. Price Action Setups

Use pattern behavior near key levels rather than random entries.

Practice: Build a playbook of your favorite setups with screenshots.

3. Risk Management

Decide risk before entry so one trade never crushes your account.

Practice: Set a fixed risk percentage and track your drawdown.

4. Confluence

Wait until two or more factors align instead of trading one signal.

Practice: Pass on trades with weak confluence even if they “look good.”

5. Momentum & Indicators

Use indicators to support the story that price is already telling.

Practice: Note when indicators confirmed vs. lagged your decision.

6. Psychology & Bias

Notice fear, FOMO, and revenge trading patterns in yourself.

Practice: Add a “mindset note” to each journal entry.

7. Trading Plan & Journal

Write your rules down and review real trade data against them.

Practice: Schedule a weekly review session for your trades.
