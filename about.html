About Nubia Leo

Who I Am

Name: Nubia Leo
Role: Creator of Leo Legacy Trading
Email: Leolegacytrades@gmail.com

I’m an Army National Guard soldier and IT student who is passionate about finance, entrepreneurship, and building systems that create long-term wealth. This project combines my technical skills and my interest in trading.

Mission Statement

My goal is to build a trading education brand that focuses on discipline over hype. I want beginners to understand how markets move, how to protect their capital, and how to think in probabilities instead of emotions.

"I don't chase money — I build systems that attract it."

Project Purpose

This website was built for IT 3203 to demonstrate responsive web design, content organization, and simple interactivity using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

It also serves as the first version of a potential personal brand and future learning hub for traders who want to improve their process, not just chase signals.
